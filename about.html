Humble beginnings

Watamu bakery was an idea that arose during a very ordinary event. This idea was conceived during one of our interacions at MYC tech Academy. We noticed that sessions were dull with everyone focused on their own tasks. Interactions were minimal. One Friday we baked some cookies and brought to class. Everyone seemed happy and this led to interactions among students and the instructors.Everyone loved the cookies and requested for more. We ended up spending more time baking more cookies and bringing them to class. This exercise made us perfect our baking skills and it helped build our first customer base.

Our main aim is to enrich the local markets by providing high quality products and maintaining excellent customer service

We believe that building and maintaining good customer relationship will be beneficial for the continued growth of our business. We strive to achieve full customer satisfaction by ensuring we deliver freshly baked products to our clients on time. We ensure that the ingredients used are from fresh raw materials from the local suppliers.Our customers' needs is our priority
